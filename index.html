About Us

VeggieRush is your local neighborhood vegetable store owned and operated by Associated Food Stores, a member-owned wholesale grocery distributor based in Delhi, India. We are committed to providing wholesome products that fit your lifestyle. That is why we continue to add to our growing list of natural, organic and gluten-free lines. It is part of living up to our promise to provide you with fresh, nutritious vegetables in convenient, easy-to-shop, locations.
